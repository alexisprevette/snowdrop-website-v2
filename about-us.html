About Us

From humble beginnings, we have grown to provide far more than meals, but we will never forget where we started. We made lifelong memories working out of our kitchens and built new friendships while serving our community for the first time. Over the years, we have expanded our reach, strengthened our programs, and continued to find new ways to make a positive impact. We are proud of what we’ve accomplished and warmly welcome anyone who wants to join us in this journey.
